    Graphic design FAQs. 

    • What graphic design materials can you do for me?

      Honestly? Anything. That’s a pretty unsatisfying answer, but there’s simply too much marketing collateral to list here. From simple business cards to delicate website graphics, car wraps, complex software mockups and lengthy annual reports, if it needs to be designed, we can more than likely do it.  


      If you're not sure whether we do something, just ask us!

    • Do you design and print physical material?

      Design? Yes. Print? No. 


      To be clear, we can design print materials, but we don't actually do the printing part. However, we'll supply all the graphic design collateral in print-ready formats with bleed marks. This way, it's ready to go when you hand it to a professional printer. Please note that your design costs with us do not cover printing costs. These are determined by the printer you use and go directly to them. 


      Don't know where to go for printing? No problem — we'll connect you with a local printer we know and trust — and have used for years ourselves. 


    • Who owns the design files?

      Once, we hand over the design files, these designs are yours — we don’t have any ownership over them. They can be freely handed to any printer or internal marketing teams you work with. 

    • Can you design a logo for me?

      Absolutely! We actually specialise in branding and rebranding. You'll find everything you need to know about our branding services here.
