    Rent-A-Website FAQs.

    • Why does a small business need a website?

      There are heaps of reasons why small business owners should have a website. The main one is, your website is a central landing point for all potential customers.


      Not everyone is ready to call straight away, so this gives customers the opportunity to do a discreet credibility check while they’re considering whether to get in touch. It's also a place where you can share your amazing services, team expertise, benefits, and points of difference.


      Your website also houses all your contact information.

    • What's included in my Rent-A -Website?

      So much! No matter which package you choose, you have an entire marketing team at your disposal — a Designer, Copywriter, and Account Manager, and an SEO Specialist. They're all dedicated to your website build throughout your partnership with us. 


      Your website will be SEO-optimised, beautifully designed, and written to be highly engaging. You have unlimited changes available to you, so we can update the website as much or as little as you like. Hosting is also included!


      To recap, here's what you get:

      • The first 30-days free & no lock-in contracts. 
      • An local Leederville-based team of website-building pros.
      • SEO.
      • Captivating graphics and layouts, your brand colours and logo incorporated, moving elements, all backed by best design practices.
      • If you don't have a logo (e.g. you're a brand-spanking new business), a basic text logo will be included at no extra charge.
      • Unlimited access to an image library with over 400 million professional stock photos.
      • Catchy headlines and highly engaging written content that informs, educates, and encourages people to take action, without the dreaded "sales push".
      • Hosting.
      • SSL certificate. 
      • Designed and optimised for desktop, tablet, and phone viewing.
      • Built in analytics.
      • Speedy local support from Leederville. 
      • Unlimited website changes and updates.

      In the spirit of open-communication, here's what's not included:

      • Purchasing and hosting your domain.
      • eCommerce and online store functionality (we  cater to that with our Business Website package).
      • Blogging, digital advertising, or graphic design beyond what goes on the website (these are done through our Marketing Essentials package). 

      That's it!

    • What's the difference between the two options?

      The only difference is the number of pages included. Our $99 + GST a month option gives you one webpage, while our $249 + GST a month option gives you an unlimited number of pages. 


      These pages are your choice — choose any combination of Home, About, Blog, Services, Team, Contact, News, Price Lists, Testimonials, Events, an Online Store, FAQ page, and more. If you're just going with one page, we can help you decide what to put on it to maximise the space you have.

    • What software is the website built in?

      Our Rent-A-Website websites are built in Duda. We have purposely chosen this CMS as it's exceptionally easy to use, edit, and looks great. We love it so much that our own Helium website — the website you're on right now — is built in Duda!

    • Can I edit the website after you build it?

      Your package includes unlimited edits from us, so there's really no reason you'd need to jump into the backend. However, we can provide editing access on request if you'd feel more comfortable with that. 


      If you do decide you'd like access, you'll be pleased to know that Duda is a code-free CMS. This means you'll never need to hire technical experts or Developers to help you. Of course, we're always available to help you if you need us (you are paying for it, after all).

    • What website maintenance will I need to do?

      None, we do it for you!

    • What happens to my site if I want to break up with Helium?

      One of the best parts of this package (and actually... all our packages!) is that there are no lock-in contracts. This means you're free to pull the pin at any time. 


      We hope this won't be the case, but we also understand that circumstances change. If you do decide to move away from Helium for whatever reason, we'll have a conversation about what will happen with your website. Seeing as your "renting" the website from us, we'll talk about either selling it to you so you have full ownership, or closing it down completely — it just depends on your circumstances and preferences.
