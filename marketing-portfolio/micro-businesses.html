    FAQ.

    • Do I need a website for my micro business?

      Honestly? Yes. The more exposure your micro business gets, the faster it'll get traction in the market. You want to get your products and services in front of as many people as possible — and a website is a great avenue for that. 


      Your micro business website is a powerful asset and will certainly make life easier. It acts as an information hub, and showcases services, case studies, and testimonials, products, and shows viewers what you bring to the table. 


      You can also direct people to your website form Google searches and social media channels to reach more people looking for exactly what you offer. 

    • How much does a micro business website cost?

      All of the websites we’ve built (so far) for micro businesses fit into our Simple Website option, coming in at $4,999 + GST. With five pages, and built by our in-house team in just one day, this is great website option for micro businesses. 


      However, we also have website packages for micro businesses that require complex functionality. These start from $10k + GST. We love transparency with this stuff, so you can see our pricing and packages here.  

    • What's the best way to build a micro business website?

      Whilst you can attempt to build a website in-house using DIY builders like Squarespace or Wix, it’s always better to hand over your biggest, and arguably most important, digital asset to a professional. We create beautiful, easy-to-edit small business websites in a platform called Duda — a great alternative to WordPress, which can be very difficult to update.

    • What should I put on my micro business website?

      As a guide, we generally suggest you include your services or products, and what makes you different to others in your sector. How do you stand out from your competitors, and what do your customers gain by choosing you? You can also include your bio, case studies, testimonials, blog articles, and more. 


      However, don’t get too caught up on this. When you engage Helium for a website, we’ll guide you on what content to include, and even write it for you. Plus, our websites are incredibly easy to edit, so they can grow and evolve along with your business. You can add, remove, update, or edit content as much as you like.
